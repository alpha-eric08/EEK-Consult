Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of honesty and accountability in all operations.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving and embracing new technologies and methodologies in ICT and engineering services.
  • Quality: Commitment to delivering services and products of the finest quality.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Ensuring client needs are met with precision and excellence.
  • Professionalism: Maintaining a high level of expertise and competence among staff and operations.

Vision

To be the market leader in ICT and engineering services by providing the highest level of technical expertise and technology to architects, engineers, building officials, government departments, cooperative institutions, and real estate developers. E.E.K. aims to remain committed to customer service, cost sensitivity, alternative solution designs, and consideration of historical and cultural significance in maintenance.

Mission

To advance the development of electrical services engineering at national and international levels by providing exceptional services and products through innovative ideas and methods.

Our Core Mandate

  • Distribution
  • Purchasing
  • Construction
  • Supervision
  • Miscellaneous

To transmit, supply, and distribute electricity and it's appliances efficiently and reliably, ensuring uninterrupted power to residential, commercial, and industrial customers. We leverage advanced technology and best practices to maintain and expand our distribution network, meeting the growing energy demands of our communities.

To procure high-quality materials, equipment, and services required for our operations. Our purchasing strategies are designed to ensure cost-effectiveness, timely delivery, and adherence to industry standards, enabling us to support our projects and services with the best resources available.

To execute construction projects with precision and excellence. Our construction mandate covers the building of electrical infrastructure, renewable energy installations, and real estate development. We are committed to safety, sustainability, and innovation in every project we undertake.

To oversee and manage all aspects of our projects and operations. Our supervision mandate ensures that all activities are conducted in compliance with regulatory requirements, industry standards, and our own stringent quality control measures. We prioritize the safety of our workforce and the satisfaction of our clients.

To provide a broad range of additional services that support our core activities and meet the diverse needs of our clients. This includes training and mentorship programs, consultancy services, and research and development initiatives. We strive to offer comprehensive solutions that contribute to the success and growth of our clients and the communities we serve.
