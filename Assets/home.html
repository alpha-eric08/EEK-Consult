E.E.K. Consults and Electricals Limited is a dynamic company specializing in electrical installations, ICT consultancy, and renewable energy solutions. Founded in June 2014 and rebranded in 2019, we are known for delivering high-quality services across various sectors, including domestic, commercial, and industrial.

Our expertise covers IT Software Development And Training, supervising, installing, and maintaining high and low voltage systems, solar installations, CCTV systems, and more. We are committed to innovation, integrity, and customer satisfaction, aiming to lead in ICT Consultancy and Engineering services by providing top-notch technical expertise.

Guided by our core values of professionalism, quality, and customer service, we strive to meet client needs with cost-effective and innovative solutions. Our vision is to be the market leader in our field, continually advancing consultancy services with the State of the Art Technology at national and international levels.
