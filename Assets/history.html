History of EEK

Foundation and Early Years (2014)

Electronic And Engineering Kits also known as EEK was founded in 2014 with a vision to transform IT and Energy landscapes through innovative solutions and sustainable practices. Originally starting as a small startup focusing on energy consultancy, EEK quickly established itself as a pioneer in the industry, earning a reputation for quality and reliability.

Expansion and Diversification (2019 - 2020)

In 2019, EEK COCSULTS changed from sole properties into a Limited as EEK CONSULTS AND ELECTRICALS LTD. It began diversifying its services beyond energy consultancy to include IT innovation and software development, responding to the growing demand for digital transformation in various sectors. This period saw the company expanding its workforce and acquiring state-of-the-art technologies to support its new ventures.

Commitment to Sustainability (2020)

EEK’s commitment to sustainability became a driving force in the mid-2020s, leading to the development of clean and renewable energy solutions. The company invested heavily in research and development in IT to create energy-efficient systems that reduced environmental impact and enhanced energy independence for clients worldwide.

Innovation and Leadership (2020 - Present)

Over the past few years, EEK has solidified its position as an industry leader by embracing cutting-edge technologies such as automation and AI. The company’s focus on electrical and electronic automations has enabled clients to improve operational efficiency and safety in industrial environments.

Looking forward, EEK remains committed to exploring new opportunities and expanding its global footprint. The company aims to drive change in the energy sector by developing innovative solutions that meet the challenges of tomorrow. With a strong foundation and a clear vision, EEK is poised to continue its legacy of excellence and impact in the years to come.
